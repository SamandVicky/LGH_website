Protocol Prayers

Protocol Prayers are Scripture-based prayers that help you approach the Father with confidence and order. They give language to your faith — declarations and petitions rooted in the finished work of Christ that you can pray over your life, family, and calling.

  • Rooted in Scripture and grace
  • Declarations and petitions to pray
  • For yourself, family, and others
  • Build a consistent prayer life
